Ethereum ETH

Ethereum is an open-source, decentralized computing platform that allows smart contracts and decentralized applications (dApps). The more poetic name of the Ethereum project is “a world computer”.

The idea behind Ethereum is to enhance opportunities for blockchain technology and to go beyond just transferring value from one to another. In 2013 it was proposed in a whitepaper by Vitalik Buterin to create a decentralized platform, that will allow users to implement smart contracts and design decentralized applications.

Ethereum’s native currency – Ether (ETH) is used as gas, which refers to fuel for the execution of smart contracts and functioning of dApps. After the London fork in the Ethereum network, when EIP-1559 was implemented, a gas-burning mechanism was launched, which caused a strong growth of the ETH price.

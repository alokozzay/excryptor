How it works

01

Select the currency exchange pair

If you would like to exchange Bitcoin to Ethereum, type in the amount of BTC you would like to exchange in the “You send” section and choose “BTC” from the drop-down menu. Then choose “ETH” from the drop-down menu in the “You receive” section and the service will automatically calculate the amount of Ethereum you’ll get. Then click the "Exchange" button.

02

Enter your wallet address

Now you need to enter the you Ethereum address in the "Your ETH Address" box. Click "Next". Your Ethereum coins will be sent to this address right after the exchange. If you don't have an Ethereum address, you can create a wallet using one of our partner services:
